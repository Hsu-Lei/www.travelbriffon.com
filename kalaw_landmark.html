Famous Landmarks in Kalaw

Done By Team Briffon












1. Green Hill Valley Elephant Camp

Green Hill Valley is a place which is caring for elephants and helping the community with awareness. This is also a place where the visitors enjoy visiting. This farm offers you to get a hands-on elephant experience.




2. Kalaw Railway Station

Kalaw Railway station is a beautiful place to get good pictures, which is located in Shan state, Myanmar. Before sunset is the perfect time to take pictures of it. This railway station is one of the English style buildings from this mountain town



3. Hnee pagoda

Hnee pagoda, also known as bamboo pagoda is home to a 500-year-old, gold-lacquered bamboo Buddha. The Buddha statue here is made of bamboo. If you love to visit historical places, I'd suggest you to visit this pagoda. It is a must go place for both local and foreign travellers.




4. Inle lake

We shouldn't leave Inle lake from this list if we're talking about kalaw. Inle lake is a wonderful place which is 57 kilometres away from Kalaw. We have many reasons why you must visit this place. The floating market is also one of its attractions. Inle is a perfect place if you're looking for something new.



5. Christ the king church

This church is an old catholic church, located on top of a hill in this beautiful town. It was built during the colonial emperor. Even though it is poorly maintained, it still has some visitors since it is located on a hill. People can see Kalaw's breathtaking views from this place.
