Famous Landmarks in Kyaik Hto

Done By Team Briffon












1.Kyaikhtiyo Pagoda

The name of the pagoda “Kyaiktiyo” derived from “Kyaik-Thi-Yo” and it was built more than 2500 years ago. Pagoda islocated on one of the mountains kyaik hto which’s at 3615 feet (1102 meters) above sea level. Its distinctive feature is that it sits uniquely at the apex of a huge boulder stone.




2. Kyaikhtiyo museum

Kyaikhtiyo museum is also a top rated tourist attraction from kyaik hto.



3. Taaingtawmu Statue of Gautama Buddha

This Pagoda is the world's second largest pagoda, located in kyaik hto, Myanmar. It was finished building on 7th April 2019.
