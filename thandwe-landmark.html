Famous Landmarks in Thandwe

Done By Team Briffon












1. Ngapali beach

Ngapali beach is located 7 km away from Thandwe, Rakhine State. Beach distinctive feature is that it has white stretch of sand dotted with palm trees, crystal-clear water, with numbers of resorts, hotels and facilities with an international standard along its extensive shore. Prices of staying in nearby resorts can be generally high. However, the best thing about staying in a beach area is that visitors can experience wonderful sightseeing and delicious local seafoods at the same time which’s worth it.




2. Ngapali Art Gallery

You should go take a look at this place if you visit Ngapali. This is a place where you would say "definitely worth popping in" after visiting.



3. Thandwe market

Thandwe market, located in the center of the town. You can find all clothes, cosmetics, shoes and household goods here, as well as produce and plenty of dried fish. basically, we can say all you need is here.




4. Kathaya beach

A little beach with quiet and beautiful scenery. Worth a visit. Kathaya beach has a really amazing environment to spend with your family or friends. It's also near the famous beach Ngapali, so don't forget this little beach in case you visit Ngapali!



5. Shwe Nandaw Pagoda Hill

Don't leave shwe Nandaw Pagoda from your conversation when you talk about "the best places for sunrise". 10 minutes drive from the local market and you will reach the pagoda. It is on the highest hill and you can see every place of thandwe from there.
